WFM · Detector Inspector10 / Schedule Adherence
Day-to-day expectation
Schedule Adherence

What is adherence?

  • Being on the right activity at the right scheduled time
  • Following your start time, break windows, and shift end exactly
  • Measured as % of shift time spent correctly on task

Why it matters

  • 1 agent off-schedule at peak can break the whole queue model
  • WFM staffing plans assume 100% schedule compliance
  • Adherence is monitored live and reported to leadership daily
Your daily shift routine — hover each step

Stay on queue between breaks

Return from breaks immediately. Any unnecessary offline time is flagged live.

Complete shift fully before clocking out

Finish wrap-up and log out on time. Early logout = undertime = affects your rate.
